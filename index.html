Description

Bacterial DNA Purification Kit is simple and rapid system for high quality bacterial genomic DNA preparation using a rapid spin column protocol. High molecular weight genomic DNA purified with the kit is suitable for direct use in all common molecular biology applications: PCR, qPCR, genotyping, sequencing and more. The protocol can be completed including incubation time in approximately 60 minutes.
